Customer Support Chat Agent (Remote/No Experience)

We are currently seeking highly motivated and reliable individuals who possess excellent communication skills and are proficient in English. As an online chat assistant, you will engage with visitors on websites through online chat and provide exceptional customer service. We are looking for friendly and personable individuals who can handle a high volume of chats and maintain a positive and professional demeanor.

In this role, you will be responsible for answering live chat messages for various businesses using messaging tools such as Facebook Messenger. As a fully remote position, you can work from anywhere with an internet connection. Successful candidates can earn up to $280 per day.

Responsibilities:

Communicate with website visitors using online messaging tools such as Facebook Messenger.
Provide exceptional customer service by answering live chat messages for various businesses.
Answer customer questions, provide sales links, and offer discounts.
Maintain a positive and professional demeanor while handling a high volume of chats.
Meet performance metrics such as response time and customer satisfaction.

Rate:

The rate for this position is $35 per hour.

Requirements:

Access to a laptop, phone, or tablet.
Reliable internet connection.
Basic English writing skills.

Skills/Background Needed:

These are beginner-level live chat jobs, so full training is provided. You do not need to have any paid live chat work experience before. We are looking for individuals who possess:

Excellent communication skills.
Strong problem-solving skills.
Ability to work in a fast-paced environment.
Ability to multitask and prioritize tasks.

Location:

This is a remote work position open to applicants worldwide. However, applicants from the United States are preferred.
