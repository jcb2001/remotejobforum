Instagram Content Manager - Earn $175 per Day and Boost Brand Engagement on Instagram


About the Role

We are seeking a creative and detail-oriented Social Media Coordinator to assist in the planning, execution, and optimization of our social media marketing efforts. This is an entry-level position, and the ideal candidate will have a passion for social media and a desire to learn and grow in the role. The Social Media Coordinator will work closely with the Social Media Manager to develop and implement social media strategies that align with our brand's goals and objectives.

Responsibilities

Create and schedule social media content across various platforms, including Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and LinkedIn
Monitor and respond to comments and messages on our social media platforms in a timely and professional manner
Assist in the development of social media campaigns and promotions
Analyze and report on social media metrics, including follower growth, engagement rates, and website traffic
Stay up-to-date with the latest social media trends and technologies and make recommendations for improvements to our social media strategies
Collaborate with other teams, such as marketing and content, to ensure consistency in messaging and branding across all channels
Requirements
Bachelor's degree in marketing, communications, or a related field (or currently pursuing)
No prior work experience required, but relevant internships or coursework is a plus
Excellent written and verbal communication skills
Strong organizational and project management skills
Familiarity with social media platforms and trends
Ability to work independently and as part of a team
Knowledge of Adobe Creative Suite is a plus
